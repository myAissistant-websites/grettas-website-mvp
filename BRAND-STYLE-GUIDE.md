# Gretta Hughes Real Estate — Brand Style Guide

A comprehensive guide to the fonts, colors, and design elements used across the Gretta Hughes website. Use this as a reference for all marketing materials, social media, print collateral, and any branded content to ensure visual consistency.

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Background (Cream)** | `#FDFCFA` | Main background, website pages |
| **Background Alt (Warm Cream)** | `#F5F0EB` | Alternate section backgrounds |
| **Dark / Text** | `#1A1A1A` | Headings, body text, dark backgrounds (footer) |
| **Muted Text** | `#6B6B6B` | Secondary text, descriptions, captions |

### Accent Colors

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Accent (Warm Taupe)** | `#B8A089` | Accent highlights, text selection |
| **Accent Light** | `#9C8570` | Italic accent phrases, hover states |
| **Gold** | `#C4A265` | Section labels, decorative lines, link hovers, premium accents |

### Supporting Colors

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Card White** | `#FFFFFF` | Card backgrounds, clean surfaces |
| **Border** | `#E8E4DF` | Subtle borders, dividers |

### Usage Tips
- The **cream/off-white backgrounds** (#FDFCFA and #F5F0EB) create a warm, inviting feel — use them as base colors for any marketing material rather than stark white.
- **Gold (#C4A265)** is the standout accent — use it sparingly for emphasis, labels, and decorative elements.
- **Dark (#1A1A1A)** is used for primary buttons and text — it's a softer alternative to pure black.
- Avoid pure black (#000000) and pure white (#FFFFFF) for large areas — always lean warm.

## Typography

### Font Families

#### Display Font — Cormorant Garamond
- **Source:** [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Cormorant+Garamond)
- **Weights used:** Light (300), Regular (400), Medium (500), SemiBold (600), Bold (700)
- **Usage:** All headings, page titles, hero text, brand statements, pull quotes
- **Character:** Elegant, sophisticated serif with high contrast strokes

#### Body Font — Outfit
- **Source:** [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Outfit)
- **Weights used:** Light (300), Regular (400), Medium (500), SemiBold (600)
- **Usage:** Body text, labels, buttons, navigation, form fields, UI elements
- **Character:** Clean, modern geometric sans-serif

### Typography Hierarchy

| Element | Font | Weight | Size | Style |
|---------|------|--------|------|-------|
| **Hero Heading** | Cormorant Garamond | Regular 400 | 60–72px | Normal |
| **Section Heading** | Cormorant Garamond | Regular 400 | 36–48px | Normal |
| **Sub-heading** | Cormorant Garamond | Regular 400 | 24–30px | Normal |
| **Accent Phrase** | Cormorant Garamond | Regular 400 | Varies | *Italic* |
| **Section Label** | Outfit | Medium 500 | 11px | UPPERCASE, wide tracking (0.3em) |
| **Body Text** | Outfit | Light 300 | 16–18px | Normal |
| **Button Text** | Outfit | Medium 500 | 12px | UPPERCASE, tracking (0.2em) |
| **Small Text / Captions** | Outfit | Regular 400 | 12–14px | Normal |

### Typography Styling Notes
- **Headings** use tight line-height (1.1) for a compact, editorial look.
- **Body text** uses relaxed line-height for comfortable reading.
- **Section labels** (e.g., "FEATURED LISTINGS", "ABOUT GRETTA") are always uppercase, gold (#C4A265), tiny (11px), and widely tracked (letter-spacing: 0.3em).
- **Italic accent** is used on select phrases within headings to add warmth — paired with the taupe accent color (#9C8570).
- For social media headers or graphics, pair a large Cormorant Garamond heading with a small Outfit label above it to match the website pattern.

## Buttons & CTAs

### Primary Button
- **Background:** Dark (#1A1A1A)
- **Text:** White, Outfit Medium, 12px, UPPERCASE
- **Letter Spacing:** 0.2em
- **Padding:** Generous (approx. 32px horizontal, 16px vertical)
- **Hover:** Slight lift effect, deeper shadow
- **Shadow:** Subtle drop shadow

### Secondary Button (Outline)
- **Border:** 1px solid, border color (#E8E4DF)
- **Text:** Dark (#1A1A1A), Outfit Medium, 12px, UPPERCASE
- **Letter Spacing:** 0.2em
- **Hover:** Border shifts to accent, slight lift

### Text Link
- **Text:** Dark (#1A1A1A), Outfit Medium, 14px, UPPERCASE
- **Letter Spacing:** 0.2em
- **Decoration:** Thin horizontal line that extends on hover

## Decorative Elements

### Gold Accent Lines
- Thin horizontal lines (1px) in gold (#C4A265) are used as decorative dividers between section labels and headings.
- Gradient dividers fade from transparent to gold and back — used in the footer and between sections.

### Section Labels Pattern
The website uses a consistent pattern for introducing sections:
1. Small gold uppercase label (e.g., "ABOUT GRETTA")
2. Short gold decorative line
3. Large serif heading

Replicate this hierarchy in marketing materials for brand consistency.

### Subtle Texture
- A very faint grain/noise texture overlay is applied across the site at near-invisible opacity — this adds warmth and prevents flat digital feel.

## Photography Style

Based on the website imagery:
- **Warm, natural lighting** — no harsh flash or cold tones
- **Professional but approachable** — lifestyle shots mixed with professional portraits
- **Consistent warm color grading** to match the cream/gold palette
- **High resolution** for both web and print use

---

## Design Principles

1. **Warm over cold** — cream instead of white, charcoal instead of black, gold instead of blue
2. **Elegant simplicity** — generous whitespace, minimal decoration, let content breathe
3. **Serif + sans-serif pairing** — Cormorant Garamond for personality, Outfit for clarity
4. **Subtle motion** — gentle hover effects and entrance animations, nothing jarring
5. **Consistent hierarchy** — every section follows label > line > heading > body pattern
6. **Premium feel** — the design language conveys luxury real estate without being ostentatious

## Quick Reference for Social Media

When creating branded social media posts:

- **Background color:** #FDFCFA (cream) or #1A1A1A (dark)
- **Heading font:** Cormorant Garamond, Regular or Light
- **Body/label font:** Outfit, Light or Medium
- **Accent color for highlights:** #C4A265 (gold)
- **Text color on light backgrounds:** #1A1A1A
- **Text color on dark backgrounds:** #FFFFFF with 60-80% opacity for secondary text
- **Section labels:** Always UPPERCASE, widely spaced, gold, tiny
- **Avoid:** Pure black backgrounds, pure white backgrounds, cold blues, heavy drop shadows, rounded/bubbly elements
